Case Study: Health Management Information System (HMIS)

Overview & Goal

  • Centralized platform for procurement & inventory across 120+ health centers
  • Replace legacy systems, enable real-time monitoring & reporting
  • Improve resource management, supply chain, and emergency response

Objectives

  • Unify 7 legacy systems into HMIS
  • Automate reporting, reduce manual work by 75%
  • Enable real-time stock visibility & automated alerts
  • Provide interactive dashboards for stakeholders

Scope & Stakeholders

  • Nationwide coverage, 120+ health centers
  • Integration with Ministry of Health systems
  • Stakeholders: MoH, UPA, facility managers, IT, logistics, analytics teams

Timeline & Phases

  • System design & legacy analysis (complete)
  • Integration & development (complete)
  • Data migration & testing (complete)
  • Dashboard customization & alert automation (in progress)
  • Go-live & feedback (upcoming)

Dependencies & Risks

  • Full data access from legacy systems
  • Timely MoH IT team participation
  • Compatibility with UPA infrastructure
