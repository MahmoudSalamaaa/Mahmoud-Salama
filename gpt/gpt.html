Our Methodologies

  • Requirement Analysis: Stakeholder engagement, scope, deliverables: BRD, SRS

  • Testing: QA, bug fixing, UAT, reports

  • Sprint Execution: Coding, integration, daily standups

  • Sprint Review: Demos, feedback, stakeholder notes

  • Release: Deployment, release notes, confirmation
