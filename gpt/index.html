Department Overview

The Department of Systems, Applications and Technical Support serves as the technological backbone of the Unified Procurement Authority (UPA), enabling efficient procurement operations through custom software development, 24/7 technical support for 50,000+ end-users, secure system integrations with government platforms, and proactive cybersecurity measures compliant with Cybersecurity standards.

Mission: Empower UPA's procurement ecosystem through reliable, secure, and innovative technology solutions that drive operational excellence.
